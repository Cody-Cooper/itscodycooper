Tacoma is a city in Pierce County, Washington, about an hour south of Seattle. It has many beautiful things to offer, like Mt. Ranier looming in the distance, the beautiful view of the Puget sound from Point Defiance and Chambers Bay, and a thriving downtown with everything you could possibly want.
